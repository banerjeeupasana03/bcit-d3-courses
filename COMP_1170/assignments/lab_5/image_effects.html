Alice In The Wonderland

Alice in The Wonderland by Lewis Carroll is one of my go-to books whenever I crave for a good read. I also enjoy watching the movie very much. It just caters to my imagination that runs wild through the pages of the book.

Here are some of the quotes that I love from the book and the movie enlisted below. They really make you wonder and transport you in the dimension of our inner child, that wants to get lost in the world of dreams and reality, coz that's where magic happens!!!

  • Chesire cat : I'm not crazy, my reality is just different than yours.
  • The Mad Hatter: Have I gone mad?
    Alice: I'm afraid so. You're entirely bonkers. But I'll tell you a secret. All the best people are.
  • “Begin at the beginning," the King said, very gravely, "and go on till you come to the end: then stop.”
  • “Who in the world am I? Ah, that's the great puzzle.”
  • “Alice:How long is forever? White Rabbit:Sometimes, just one second.”
  • “If you don't know where you are going any road can take you there”
